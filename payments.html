PAYMENT DETAILS

IF NO PAYMENT IS RECEIVED ON THE FIRST WEEK BACK AN ADMINISTRATION FEE OF £10.00 WILL BE ADDED TO YOUR BILL.

Waves accept cash or cheque, but our preferred method of payment is online. Clients should contact us directly for more details.

Lessons run only in school term time. Fees will be determined by amount of weeks in the term. Families with 3 or more children attending swimming lessons will receive 50p per lesson per child reduction.

On the penultimate week of the swimming term all swimmers will be given a priority slip with next term dates, class time and payment details. If you wish to continue your lessons on the next course you will need to pay on the date shown.

If on that day you are unable to pay for the next course, but you would like your child to continue lessons, you must leave a message or phone the appropriate teacher (Contact Waves) otherwise your place may be filled.

Lessons on Friday and Saturday cheques payable to ‘Saturday Waves’

HOLIDAYS

THERE WILL BE NO REFUND MADE FOR THOSE UNABLE TO ATTEND LESSONS.
