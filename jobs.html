Job Vacancies

Waves Swim School will consider all swimming teachers who hold an ASA level 1/2 (or STA equivalent), and lifeguards who hold a current RLSS certificate. All applicants will undergo a standard DBS check.

Applicants must be able to demonstrate strong teaching skills, and be capable of teaching all ages with enthusiasm. A strong work ethic, and the ability to work well in a team are desirable.

Waves offer competitive fees for teachers and lifeguards. If you feel you are interested in a job with Waves, please get in contact (See: Contact Waves).
