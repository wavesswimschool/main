Mission Statement

Waves Swimming School will endeavour to provide access to swimming tuition for children and adults from all areas of the Community using the nationally recognised swimming programmes from Swim England, STA and RLSS.

The School will provide meaningful and appropriate experiences that can be enjoyed by all and which recognise and value the diversity of participants. It will encourage high expectations and standards of achievement from all swimmers and ensure they are able to reach their full potential.

The School will ensure that swimming staff are suitably trained to recognised standards as designated by Swim England, STA and RLSS organisations and via the system of continuous professional development.
